The Process

  • Source

    We go out of our way to personally pick and evaluate different loose leaf teas from around the world to include in our boxes.

  • Deliver

    At the end of every month we pack a variety of teas, along with the description and brewing instruction of different teas.

  • Enjoy

    You can sit back and enjoy the teas we've curated for you. If you like the tea you can arrange to purchase more via contacting us.
